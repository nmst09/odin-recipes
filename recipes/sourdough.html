Sourdough

Description

This sourdough bread is not complicated but it will take time, attention to detail and patience.This beginner sourdough recipe is perfect for bakers looking to jump right in! Itâ€™s is a low-hydration dough, meaning it will yield a tight crumb (small holes). It is great for sandwiches and toast.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough Starter
  • Warm Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Bread Flour
  • Sea Salt
  • Ground Cornmeal

Directions

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Bulk Rise
  3. Stretch and fold the dough.
  4. Cut and shape the dough.
  5. Second rise.
  6. Score the dough.
  7. Bake the dough.
