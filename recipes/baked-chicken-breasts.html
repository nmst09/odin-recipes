Baked Chicken Breasts

Description

Baked chicken that's tender, juicy, and perfect every time with this simple, 5-ingredient recipe for boneless, skinless chicken breasts. Adding just a bit of chicken broth to those beautiful pan drippings creates a tasty pan sauce that adds extra flavor at the table.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Creole Seasoning
  • Chicken Broth

Directions

  1. Coat chicken breasts with olive oil.
  2. Sprinle both siides of breasts with salt and creole seasoning.
  3. Bake in oven for 10mins and then flip and bake for another 15mins
  4. Pour the chicken broth in the pan used to cook the chicken and scrape any browned bits off of the pan.
  5. Drizzle chicken broth over the cooked chicken.
